Coron Beach, Palawan


Coron Island in Palawan is world-famous for its dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear lagoons, and stunning lakes that seem hidden within towering rock formations. Its most iconic spot, Kayangan Lake, is often called the cleanest lake in Asia, with emerald waters perfect for swimming and snorkeling. Adventurers flock to Twin Lagoon for its enchanting mix of warm and cold waters, while divers are drawn to Coron's unique World War II shipwreck sites, now transformed into vibrant coral gardens teeming with marine life. Beyond the underwater wonders, Coron offers peaceful island-hopping, white-sand beaches, and panoramic views that showcase the raw, untouched beauty of Palawan. With its mix of natural wonders and rich history, Coron Island is truly one of the Philippines' most spectacular paradise destinations.
