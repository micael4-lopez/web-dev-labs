Salagdoong Beach, Siquijor

Salagdoong Beach, located in Maria, Siquijor, is one of the island's most popular and exciting destinations. Famous for its clear turquoise waters, soft white sand, and vibrant marine life, it's a perfect spot for swimming, snorkeling, and simply relaxing by the shore. The highlight of Salagdoong is its cliff diving platforms, where thrill-seekers can leap from heights of 7 to 10 meters straight into the refreshing sea — an adrenaline-pumping experience surrounded by stunning coastal views. Families and casual travelers can also enjoy its calm swimming areas, shaded picnic spots, and nearby restaurants. With its blend of adventure and relaxation, Salagdoong Beach captures the fun and beauty of Siquijor, making it a must-visit for anyone exploring the “Island of Fire.”
