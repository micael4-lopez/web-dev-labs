El Nido Beach, Palawan

Palawan is widely considered one of the Philippines' most scenic islands. El Nido and Coron are its most popular destinations, known for stunning limestone karsts, hidden lagoons and excellent snorkeling and diving. Island-hopping tours in Bacuit Bay (El Nido) and the coral gardens around Coron are a must. (Tip: book boat tours early in the morning to avoid crowds.)
